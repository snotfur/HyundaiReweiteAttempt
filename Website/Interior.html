      • BlueLink

      The only thing missing: an enormous price tag.

      One-touch power tilt-and-slide sunroof with sliding sunshade, dual front automatic temperature control, navigation and an audio system decked out with XM® Satellite Radio, iPod/USB and auxiliary input jacks. Plenty of luxury, at a price that makes sense.

      Stylish and Safe

      The radio and temperature controls are placed high on the dashboard for easy visibility, while buttons and knobs are generously sized and clearly marked. The gear shifter and steering-wheel-mounted cruise and audio controls are conveniently located so you can operate them by touch alone. Even the cupholders are illuminated in a soft blue glow at night to highlight their location. Each feature is designed to be visually appealing, but not visually distracting.

      Details, Details

      Featuring Bluetooth® hands-free phone system, steering-wheel-mounted audio controls and a one-touch power tilt-and-slide sunroof with sliding sunshade. Santa Fe designers even added small conveniences like a sunglass holder in the overhead console and trip computer meant to make your experience as comfortable and clutter-free as possible.

      Cargo Space

      Though Santa Fe has more passenger volume than the RAV4 and CX-7, it’s not always how much cargo space you have, but what you do with it. Inside you’ll find plenty of cubbies for storing smaller items, additional storage under the cargo floor. Within LIMITED V6 models there is a two-prong 115-volt outlet that’ll power standard household appliances like laptops and radios.

      Power Tilt-and-Slide Sunroof

      Get your daily dose of fresh air and Vitamin D with the available one-touch open power tilt-and-slide sunroof with sliding sunshade.

      Dual Front Automatic Temperature Control

      Temperature autonomy makes for happier passengers. Santa Fe LIMITED has dual front automatic temperature controls with a CleanAir Ionizer and eight fan speed settings so you can dial in the perfect temp. And unlike most competitors, Santa Fe also features B-pillar-mounted adjustable rear vents that direct the air throughout the cabin rather than just at the center passenger’s legs.

      Audio System

      The powerful audio system comes with standard steering-wheel-mounted audio controls, integrated Bluetooth® hands-free phone system and iPod®/USB and auxiliary input jacks. The LIMITED adds the Infinity® LOGIC7™ Premium Audio System with 10 speakers and an external amp that cranks out 605 watts of high-fidelity.

      Navigation System

      The Santa Fe navigation system has a large, high-resolution 6.5-inch touchscreen that’s simple, intuitive and features a rearview camera for tight parking spaces. You can search from over five million points of interest, view most recently visited locations, marked points and favorites. With built-in XM® NavTraffic™, you’re able to get real-time traffic updates to help you save time on your commute.
